About Us

We Believe in the Healing Power of Dogs

Sheltie Shenanigans is a passionate therapy dog team. Our Shetland Sheepdogs are carefully trained and evaluated to provide animal-assisted support in a wide range of settings.

  • All dogs professionally trained and evaluated for therapy work
  • Handlers trained in safe, professional animal-assisted interactions
  • Gentle, friendly Shelties suited for diverse populations
  • Committed to consistency, reliability, and meaningful connections
  • Serving Central Florida and surrounding areas

What We Do

Therapy Dog Services

We partner with organizations to bring structured, purposeful animal-assisted visits that make a real difference.

🏥

Hospital & Healthcare Visits

We visit patients, healthcare workers, and staff in hospitals, rehabilitation centers, and medical facilities to promote emotional healing and reduce anxiety.

🏫

School & University Programs

Stress-relief visits during exam periods, reading programs for children, and social-emotional learning support for students of all ages.

🏡

Senior Living Communities

Regular visits to assisted living, memory care, and nursing home communities to provide companionship and spark joyful memories.

💼

Corporate Wellness Events

Bring the Shenanigans to your office! We offer one-time or recurring workplace wellness visits to boost morale and reduce employee stress.

🎗

Crisis & Disaster Response

Trained to provide comfort at crisis sites, community events, and disaster response scenarios in partnership with local agencies.

📅

Special Events & Programs

Fundraisers, community fairs, awareness events — we love showing up where dogs and people can connect and celebrate together.
